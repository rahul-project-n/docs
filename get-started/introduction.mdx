## Implementation Guide

Follow these steps to implement Granica Crunch in your data environment.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Your Integration">
    Select the integration that best fits your existing data infrastructure, whether it's Apache Spark, Google Cloud Dataproc, or others.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Data Sources">
    Connect your data sources and configure the appropriate table formats (Hive, Delta, or Iceberg) based on your requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up Processing Mode">
    Decide between batch or streaming processing depending on your use case and start transforming your data with Granica Crunch.
  </Step>
</Steps>
